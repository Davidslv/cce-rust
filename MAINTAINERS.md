## Becoming a maintainer

There is no fixed quota. The path is earned trust:

1. Contribute quality PRs that respect the three quality gates
   (test / clippy / fmt), the TDD discipline, and spec conformance — see
   [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
2. Help with triage: reproducing bugs, reviewing others' PRs, improving docs.
3. Show sustained, reliable judgement over time — especially care about not
   drifting behaviour away from [`SPEC.md`](SPEC.md) and the Ruby sibling
   ([davidslv/cce-ruby](https://github.com/davidslv/cce-ruby)).

When a contributor is consistently doing maintainer-shaped work, the maintainer
will invite them and add them to this file. Commit access comes with the
responsibility to uphold the gates and the spec-as-constitution model.
